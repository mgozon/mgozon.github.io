About

I am a student at the University of Michigan studying mathematics and computer science. I am interested in understanding the theoretical aspect of things and why they work, but also how they can be applied for the benefit of society.


I also like to solve puzzles, usually through math and cs competitions such as the AMC, AIME, and USACO besides many others, and I am currently on UM's Competitive Programming Team. I had previously been the head of the math and cs club at Pioneer, and many guides, documents, and other materials can be found in the Resources tab.
